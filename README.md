## 📋 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Excel dashboard** I built to explore and understand sales performance across different categories, regions, and customer segments.

The goal was to transform raw transactional data into something more meaningful—making it easier to quickly see trends, compare performance over time, and identify where growth is happening.

Rather than working through spreadsheets manually, this dashboard provides a structured and visual way to understand the business at a glance.

---

## 🎯 Analytical Objectives

- Compare **current year vs previous year** sales and profit  
- Understand performance across **product categories** (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology)  
- Analyze differences across **customer segments** (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)  
- Identify **top-performing and underperforming states**  
- Track **quarterly trends in sales and pricing**  
- Provide a clear and interactive view for decision-making  

---

## 📌 Key Features

- **KPI Cards**
  - Total Sales (Previous Year vs Current Year)  
  - Total Profit (Previous Year vs Current Year)  
  - Growth Margins  

- **Interactive Filters**
  - Product Categories  
  - Customer Segments  
  - Shipping Modes
  - Top 10 Countries performing the most
  - Top 10 Countries underperforming the most
  - Clearing all filters

- **Performance Tables**
  - Sub-category breakdown with year-over-year comparison  
  - State-level analysis with growth indicators  

- **Visual Insights**
  - Quarterly sales and price trends  
  - Top-performing states by total sales  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- There is clear **growth in both sales and profit**, indicating overall business improvement  
- Some product categories, especially in technology, show **strong and consistent performance**  
- A few states contribute significantly to total sales, showing **regional concentration**  
- Sales patterns suggest **seasonal trends**, with certain quarters outperforming others  
- Growth margins highlight areas where **efficiency and profitability can be improved**
